# Nei standard genetic distance

$$-\ln \frac{\langle x, y \rangle}{||x||_2 \cdot ||y||_2}$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_s$

## Description

The Nei standard genetic distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the formula above. Under the infinite-alleles model of evolution (IAM), $D_s$ increases linearly with time.
