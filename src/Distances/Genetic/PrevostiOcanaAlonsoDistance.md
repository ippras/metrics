# Prevosti–Ocana–Alonso distance

$$\frac{\sum |x_{ij} - y_{ij}|}{2n}$$

## Description

The Prevosti–Ocana–Alonso distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the normalized Manhattan metric of their allele frequencies.
