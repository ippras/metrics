# Nei–Tajima–Tateno distance

$$1 - \frac{1}{n} \sum \sqrt{x_{ij} y_{ij}}$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_A$

## Description

The Nei–Tajima–Tateno distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the formula above.
