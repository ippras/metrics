# Dps distance

$$-\ln \frac{\sum \min\{x_{ij}, y_{ij}\}}{\sum_{j=1}^n m_j}$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_{ps}$

## Description

The Thorpe similarity (proportion of shared alleles) between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by $\sum \min\{x_{ij}, y_{ij}\}$. The Dps distance between $x$ and $y$ is defined by the negative logarithm of the normalized Thorpe similarity.
