# MHC genetic dissimilarity

## Abbreviations

- MHC
- HLA

## Description

The MHC genetic dissimilarity of two individuals is defined as the number of shared alleles in their MHC (major histocompatibility complex). MHC is the most gene-dense and fast-evolving region of the mammalian genome. In humans, it is a 3.6 Mb region containing 140 genes on chromosome 6 and called HLA (human leukocyte antigen system). HLA has the largest polymorphism (allelic diversity) found in the population.
