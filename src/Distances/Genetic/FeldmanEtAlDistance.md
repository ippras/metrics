# Feldman et al. distance

$$\log\left(1 - \frac{\sum_i (\delta \mu)_i^2}{M}\right)$$

## Description

The Feldman et al. distance is defined by the formula above, where the summation is over loci and $M$ is the average value of the distance at maximal divergence.
