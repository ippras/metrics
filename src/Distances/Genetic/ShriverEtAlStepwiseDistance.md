# Shriver et al. stepwise distance

$$D_{SW} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^n \sum_{1 \le i, j \le m_k} |i - j| (2x_{ik} y_{jk} - x_{ik} x_{jk} - y_{ik} y_{jk})$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_{SW}$

## Description

The Shriver et al. stepwise distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the formula above.
