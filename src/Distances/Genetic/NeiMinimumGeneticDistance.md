# Nei minimum genetic distance

$$\frac{1}{2n} \sum (x_{ij} - y_{ij})^2$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_m$

## Description

The Nei minimum genetic distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the formula above.
