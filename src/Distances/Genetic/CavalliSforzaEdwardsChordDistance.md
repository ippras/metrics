# Cavalli-Sforza–Edwards chord distance

$$\frac{2\sqrt{2}}{\pi n} \sum_{j=1}^n \sqrt{1 - \sum_{i=1}^{m_j} \sqrt{x_{ij} y_{ij}}}$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_{CH}$

## Description

The Cavalli-Sforza–Edwards chord distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the formula above. The related Cavalli-Sforza arc distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by $\frac{2}{\pi} \arccos \left( \sum \sqrt{x_{ij} y_{ij}} \right)$.
