# Roger distance

$$\frac{1}{\sqrt{2n}} \sum_{j=1}^n \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{m_j} (x_{ij} - y_{ij})^2}$$

## Abbreviations

- $D_R$

## Description

The Roger distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the formula above, representing a measure of genetic similarity and distance.
