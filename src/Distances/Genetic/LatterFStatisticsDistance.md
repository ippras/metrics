# Latter F-statistics distance

$$\theta^* = \frac{\sum (x_{ij} - y_{ij})^2}{2(n - \langle x, y \rangle)}$$

## Abbreviations

- $\theta^*$
- $D_L$

## Description

The Latter F-statistics distance between populations $x$ and $y$ is defined by the $F_{ST}$-estimator $\theta^*$. The Latter distance $D_L$ is defined as $-\ln(1 - \theta^*)$.
